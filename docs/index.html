GenreTV Help

Help for browsing the schedule, managing your list, publishing, and contributing to GenreTV.
Get startedOpen GenreTV

Find something to watch

Understand Now Showing, Upcoming, Awaiting Renewal or Cancellation, and Finished, then narrow each section with search and filters.

Build your list

Sign in to add, edit, or hide Shows and Seasons while keeping GenreTV’s Canonical List as your baseline.

Publish and import

Apply to publish a snapshot of your list, or bring Seasons from another published list into your own.

Improve the canonical schedule

Publishers can send proposals; maintainers can inspect differences and selectively merge trusted changes.

GenreTV keeps its working database in your browser. Start with Welcome to GenreTV or go directly to the schedule.
